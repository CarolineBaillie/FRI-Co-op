FRI Co-op

Do you want to talk to others of the same race in a safe and comfortable space?
Do you want to learn what you can do to help fight racism as a white ally?
Do you want to take action against racial injustice?
Turns out your not alone...

As citizens, we have acknowledged peoples desire for these 3 things. That's why we created FRI Co-op (Fighting Racial Injustice Co-operative), a website committed to racial education, communication, and action. Through FRI Co-op we lead weekly affinity space meetings as well as provide access to many other resources and events.
